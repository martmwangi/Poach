POACHING MENACE

Though the numbers of poaching have decreased significantly over the last 3 years, poaching still exist and its key contributor is corruption and lack of awareness. Lack of awareness of such activities could equally be summed up to ignorance. Asia have now launched a campaing to help stop demand of animal products in a bid to try to end poaching.
